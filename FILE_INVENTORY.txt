## 关键路径

### 用户认证流程
Login.tsx → authService.login() → authStore → API: /auth/login
      ↓
App.tsx (checkAuth) → MainLayout → Dashboard

### 项目查看流程
Dashboard → Projects → ProjectList.tsx
  ↓
projectService.getProjects()
  ↓
API: /projects?skip=0&limit=20
  ↓
DataTable展示

### 任务执行流程
TaskList → pipelineService.executePipeline()
  ↓
API: /pipelines/execute
  ↓
Celery后端任务
  ↓
WebSocket: task_update → TaskList实时更新
